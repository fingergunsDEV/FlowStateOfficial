About FlowState

FlowState is an electronic music artist hailing from the vibrant scene of Los Angeles, California. Known for intricate sound design, driving rhythms, and captivating melodies, FlowState creates immersive sonic journeys that bridge the gap between euphoric highs and introspective depths. Drawing inspiration from the energy of the LA nights and the vastness of the digital frontier, FlowState's music is a testament to the power of sound to transport the listener to another dimension.
